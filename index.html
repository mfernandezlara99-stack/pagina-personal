ABOUT MARIANA

From Patagonia to the Swiss Alps.

I grew up surrounded by mountains in Patagonia and today I live in the Swiss Alps, working as a ski and snowboard instructor while building Proyecto Alpino.

What started as a passion for the outdoors slowly became a way of creating meaningful experiences for other people.

I love combining adventure, nature, local culture and personalized recommendations to help travelers experience Switzerland beyond the typical tourist path.

PROYECTO ALPINO

More than guiding. A different way to experience the Alps.

Proyecto Alpino is the brand through which we create personalized mountain experiences in Switzerland.

The idea was born from a simple belief: the best adventures happen when they feel real, personal and connected to the place.

Through skiing, hiking, biking and local recommendations, we help travelers experience the Alps beyond the classic tourist version.
