ABOUT

Alpine experiences created by someone who actually lives them.

I’m Mariana — ski and snowboard instructor, outdoor lover and founder of Proyecto Alpino.

Originally from Patagonia and currently based in Switzerland, I spend most of my time between mountains, trails, snow and alpine villages.

Through Proyecto Alpino, I create personalized experiences for travelers looking for something more authentic than a typical tour.

The goal is simple: helping people experience the Alps in a more real, local and meaningful way.
